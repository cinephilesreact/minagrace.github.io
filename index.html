what is EcoKnow?

EcoKnow is an interactive, fun workshop that allows the audience to learn or "know" the importance of proper waste disposal that will serve as an eye-opener that will enhance their skills in making decisions on how it will affect the environment via the internet.

Attending our workshop is essential because pollution (water pollution, land pollution, etc.) is a dangerous threat to humans, especially in our environment. Waste mismanagement may cause different kinds of pollution that may affect us and the environment itself severely. Air pollution triggers lung diseases or illnesses like cough, asthma, etc. Meanwhile, water pollution roots the death of marine life and makes them endangered. These are some of the effects of waste mismanagement. Therefore, attending our workshop will give you the knowledge to help lessen these effects, but with lots of fun.
